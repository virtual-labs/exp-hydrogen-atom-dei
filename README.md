### About the Experiment 

This experiment explores the quantum-mechanical model of the hydrogen atom by solving Schrödinger’s equation to obtain its discrete energy levels and electron wavefunctions. It demonstrates how quantum numbers characterize the atom’s states and explains the quantized spectral lines observed experimentally.
